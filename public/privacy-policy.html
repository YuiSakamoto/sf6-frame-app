Privacy Policy

📋 Introduction

SF6 Frame Data ("the App") is a Street Fighter 6 frame data viewer and punish finder available on iOS, Android, and the web. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

🔒 Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

The following data may be collected automatically through third-party services:

  • Usage analytics — Anonymous usage data (screens viewed, features used) via Firebase Analytics to improve the App.
  • Advertising data — Device advertising identifiers and general usage data via Google AdMob to serve ads. On iOS, the App requests your consent via the App Tracking Transparency framework before accessing the advertising identifier.
  • Crash data — Anonymous crash reports to help identify and fix bugs.

📱 Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (selected character, language setting) locally on your device using MMKV storage. This data is never transmitted to any server.

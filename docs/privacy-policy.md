# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 1, 2026**

## Overview

SF6 Frame Data ("the App") is a free tool for viewing Street Fighter 6 frame data and finding punish options. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device
- **Your character preference** (selected "my character")
- **Language preference**
- **Cached frame data** for offline use

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

### Analytics and Advertising
- The App may use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements on mobile platforms (iOS/Android)
- The App may use **Google AdSense** to display advertisements on the web platform
- These services may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- On iOS, the App requests your permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before using your device's advertising identifier

### No Server-Side Data Collection
- We do not operate any servers that collect or store your personal information
- Frame data updates are fetched from GitHub's public CDN

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Google AdMob | Mobile advertising | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this document with an updated date.

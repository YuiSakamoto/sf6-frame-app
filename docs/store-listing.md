# Store Listing

## App Name
SF6 Frame Data

## Short Description (80 chars)
Frame data viewer & punish finder for Street Fighter 6

## Full Description

View frame data for all 28 characters in Street Fighter 6 and instantly find punish options when blocking your opponent's moves.

**Features:**
- Complete frame data for all 28 characters (startup, active, recovery, on block, on hit, damage)
- Punish finder: select an opponent's move and see which of your moves can punish it
- Detailed move data: combo scaling, drive gauge, SA gauge, cancel options, and properties
- Category filters: normal, unique, special, super, throw, common
- Sort punishes by startup speed or damage
- Works offline with bundled data, auto-syncs updates in the background
- Available in 14 languages: English, Japanese, French, Italian, German, Spanish, Latin American Spanish, Portuguese (Brazil), Korean, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Arabic, Polish, Russian

**Data Source:**
Frame data sourced from the official Capcom Street Fighter 6 website.
